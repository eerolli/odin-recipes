Lasagna

Description:

An Italian classic, perfect for weekend family dinners.

Pasta, cheese, meaty tomato sauce and cheese, this dish has everything you'd ever need!

Ingredients

Meat sauce:

  • 400g minced meat
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 500g crushed tomatoes
  • 100 ml water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 1 teaspoon thyme

Cheese sauce:

  • 40g butter
  • 100ml flour
  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • sprinkle of black pepper
  • 100g grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Sear the meat and onions in oil
  2. Add tomatoes, water, salt and black pepper. Let simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add oregano and thyme
  4. For the cheese sauce melt butter in a kettle and mix with flour.
  5. Pour in half of the milk. Heat and mix evenly.
  6. Pour rest of the milk. Stir and boil for two minutes.
  7. Add salt, black pepper and grated cheese
  8. Butter a tray. Pour meat sauce on the bottom, spread lasagna sheets on top.
  9. Pour cheese sauce and meat sauce on lasagna sheets. Repeat for two layers
  10. On top the last layer of sheets, pour only cheese sauce. Finish with grated cheese
  11. Cook in 200 degrees Celsius for 30-40 minutes
